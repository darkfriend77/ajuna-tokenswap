How to Wrap (AJUN → wAJUN)

  1. Enter the amount of AJUN you want to wrap
  2. Click "Approve" to allow the Wrapper contract to pull your AJUN
  3. Click "Wrap" to lock AJUN and receive wAJUN

How to Unwrap (wAJUN → AJUN)

  1. Enter the amount of wAJUN you want to unwrap
  2. Click "Approve" to allow the Wrapper contract to burn your wAJUN
  3. Click "Unwrap" to burn wAJUN and receive your AJUN back
